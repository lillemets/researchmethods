Section 3 Frequentist vs bayesian approach

.pull-left[Frequentist

  • Traditional
  • What’s the probability of data given some estimate?
  • Prior information is not relevant
  • Single parametric inference ]

.pull-right[Bayesian

  • Modern application
  • What’s the probability of an estimate?
  • Construction of a prior
  • Multiplication of an inference to get a posterior estimate ]

We will focus on the Frequentist approach.

3.1 Jamovi

To apply the methods we learn, we use Jamovi. It’s a statistical spreadsheet that is similar to other statistical packages, most notably SPSS.

3.2 R and RStudio

Based on S language orginating from 1970’s. Developed during 1990’s and became public around 2000.

3.2.1 Language and environment

Programming language similar to any other but developed paricularly for data analysis Flexible and extensible environment as opposed to many statistical packages Command line interface

3.2.2 Statistical computing and graphics

Includes many statistical procedures for various fields Constantly extended by the community with novel methods Abundant possibilities for plotting data
